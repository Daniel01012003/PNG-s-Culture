Papua New Guinea's
Brief History

Ancient Times

People have lived in PNG for over 50,000 years. PNG is also one of the most diverse countries in the world with over 800 languages spoken! It is also known for its early agricultural development or ancient farming that began in the highlands around 7,000 years ago.

Colonial Times

The First Europeans to come to PNG were (Spanish & Portuguese) in the 1500s. In the 1800s: Germany took the northern part (German New Guinea). Britain, later Australia, controlled the south (Papua). After World War I, Australia took control of both parts.
